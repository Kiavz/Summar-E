Persuasion

"Persuasion" is the last finished novel of author Jane Austen. It was published on the 20th of December, 1817. The story is about a second chance, the reawakening of love between Anne Elliot and Captain Frederick Wentworth.

Summary

Eight years after breaking off her engagement, Anne finds herself in reduced circumstances. Her family, the Elliots, is in financial trouble, thus they decide to rent out their estate, Kellynch Hall, to Admiral Croft and his wife.

The Crofts' brought Captain Wentworth with them upon their arrival. Anne finds herself still in love with the man but assumes that he has long moved on.

As the story unfolds, their misunderstandings become clarified as they confront their feelings for one another.

In the end, they find their way back to each other and resolve their miscommunications and misunderstandings.

The novel is about second chances, and it explores the tension and unspoken feelings between Anne and Wentworth.
