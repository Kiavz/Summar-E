Summary

After Emma has successfully matched her friend, Miss Taylor, with Mr. Weston, she becomes convinced of her matchmaking abilities and starts matching or pairing up her other friends with other people. However, because of Emma's efforts, the relationships resulted in a series of misunderstandings, miscommunication, and romantic entanglements.

And though Emma likes involving herself in other people's affairs, she remains oblivious about her feelings about her close family friend and neighbor, Mr. Knightly.
